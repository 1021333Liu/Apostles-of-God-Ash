# ART_ROUND_20260610

## Problems Addressed

- Player, enemies, and Boss need clearer silhouettes at 1280x720.
- Player weapon and stomach sigil need to remain readable at small screen size.
- Empty One needs to read as a broken famine villager, not a generic melee unit.
- Famine Farmer needs a clear seed-cast windup instead of a normal weapon swing.
- Hungry Scarecrow needs a taller silhouette and stronger cast-wave anticipation.
- Barn King needs stronger phase contrast and a clearly targetable red core.
- Log fragments need to read as collectible narrative samples, not generic light orbs.
